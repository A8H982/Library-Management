Who we are

About the library

The GJ Library is the college’s main learning commons for students and faculty. We support coursework and independent reading with a lending collection, a quiet reading area, and reference holdings across the arts, sciences, commerce, and regional studies—including Bihar and Patna-related titles where available.

Our mission is simple: help every reader find the right book at the right time. Library staff maintain accession records, shelf locations, and loan status so that borrowed copies are tracked fairly and returned copies go back on the shelf quickly. We also assist with locating alternative editions, suggesting related authors, and navigating the online catalog when you search from home or the campus Wi‑Fi.

Beyond textbooks, the library encourages wider reading—fiction, essays, general knowledge, and competitive-exam material where stocked—so students can build habits that last beyond a single semester. Faculty may consult us for course reserves, reading lists, and guidance on what the library can order or hold for class use.

The library is located on campus at Rambagh, Bihta, Patna. Hours and desk availability are listed in the contact section below; for special needs (extended reference, project work, or accessibility), please speak to the librarian during desk hours or use the message form there.

What we offer

Services

Borrowing, study space, and help finding information—backed by a catalog that reflects what is on the shelf, on loan, or in reference.

Borrowing & returns

Issue and return books at the circulation desk with clear due dates and renewal rules as announced each term. Overdue reminders help keep the collection circulating fairly. Staff record loans and returns in the system and update the catalog so availability and shelf locations stay accurate.

Reading space

A calm area for individual study, silent reading, and short reference sessions. Newspapers and magazines may be available for same-day reading in the library. Course-related reserves—where faculty have placed limited copies for in-library use—are handled according to staff instructions at the desk.

Reference & information help

Ask for help locating a title by author, subject, or accession number, or for suggestions when you are starting a topic. Staff can guide you to the right stack, the catalog search, and basic reference sources. For deep research, we can point you to suitable books and how to use them alongside your syllabus.

Online catalog & discovery

Browse the library catalog from this website to see titles, authors, ISBNs, shelf locations, and copy status before you visit. Cover images and details are maintained by staff so you can recognize editions and plan which books to borrow or consult during your next trip to the campus.
