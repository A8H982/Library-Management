About the library

The library supports students and faculty with lending, a reading room, and reference collections in arts, sciences, and regional studies. Staff maintain the catalog, track copies on loan, and help readers find what they need.

Services

Borrowing & returns

Issue and receive books with clear due dates. Staff use the online catalog to update availability and shelf locations.

Reading space

A calm area for individual study and reference use, with newspapers and course-related reserves when available.

Student directory

Staff choose the program (duration is set automatically), then admission year. Session shows as a range like 2023-26 (admission + passout per course length). The admission year is stored in Supabase.
